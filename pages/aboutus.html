Let's meet our team

Hadde Nakawesi & Lukiya Nalumu

We are Hadde and Lukiya, and we are passionate about helping you look and feel your best. As dedicated beauticians, we are here to create beauty, confidence, and artistry in your life.

Our Journey:

Our love for beauty began at a young age, and it evolved into a career driven by artistry and a commitment to enhancing natural features. With over 15 years of experience in the beauty industry, We've had the privilege of working with a diverse range of clients, each with unique needs and desires.

Approach:

We believe that beauty isn't one-size-fits-all. It's about celebrating your individuality and enhancing your best features. Whether it's makeup artistry to bring out your inner glow or hairstyling to capture the essence of your personality, our goal is to make you feel and look your absolute best.

What We Offer:

Makeup Services:

From everyday looks to special occasions, we specialize in creating makeup looks that reflect your style and highlight your natural beauty.

Hair Styling:

Whether you're looking for a chic haircut, elegant updo, or intricate braids, we are here to make your hair dreams a reality.

Consultation:

We take our time to understand your preferences and work with you to achieve the look you desire.

Education:

We are dedicated to share our knowledge with you. Learn makeup tips, haircare routines, and more through our tutorials and guidance.

Client-Centered:

Your satisfaction and comfort are our top priorities. We are committed to create a relaxed and enjoyable experience, ensuring that you leave feeling more confident and beautiful than ever.

Tell us a secret

We look forward to hearing from you and assisting you with all your beauty needs. Your satisfaction and input are important to us.
